About Idea Vault

Idea Vault is your automated knowledge garden that continuously curates insights from the web. Running twice daily, it fetches content, processes it intelligently, and presents it in a beautiful, dark-themed format.

How it works: Automated scraping at 08:00 & 20:00 UTC | LLM-powered content processing | Beautiful markdown reports | Zero maintenance required
